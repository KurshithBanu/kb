ENTER INTO THE MAGICAL WORLD "HARRY POTTER"

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling. The films are imaginative, adventurous, funny, frightening, and magical! They combine action, fantasy, and friendship, making them successful among children and young adults. The main character, Harry, is kind, brave, and intelligent. His journey spans eight movies, starting with Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone and ending with Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 2.
